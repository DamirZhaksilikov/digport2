Introductory excerpt from We do it all the time
To dynamically generate the film, a program parses a JSON input containing my Google Chrome history. From these entries, the program identifies the most recent links to YouTube videos whose titles contain keywords relating to crises. These videos are then downloaded and collaged into the film.
Installation setup––We do it all the time is streamed on a shattered windshield above pieces from a car interior.
For the installation and screening of We do it all the time a series of car interior parts were created based on clips that were found in previous iterations of the film. This particular piece is a headrest upholstered with teddy bear skins that resembled stuffed animals placed at the memorial of Dimitris Christoulas, a Greek pensioner who committed suicide in protest of austerity measures the government took after the '08 financial crisis.
